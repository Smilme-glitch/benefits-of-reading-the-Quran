Reading the Quran daily, especially after Fajr, has numerous benefits:

1. Spiritual growth and connection with Allah.

2. Peace and tranquility in the heart.

3. Guidance and wisdom for daily life.

4. Increased faith and devotion.

5. A sense of calm and focus for the day ahead.
